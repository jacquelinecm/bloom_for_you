Awareness

Accoring to City of Hope Breast cancer is a disease in which breast tissue cells start growing abnormally and uncontrollably. In the U.S., breast cancer is the second most common cancer in women after skin cancer. It can occur in both men and women, but it is rare in men..

Breast Cancer is very common and there are more than 200,000 cases per year in US

Self Care

Make sure that you eat healthy organic food. Take naps often as it is restorative. Make sure that you cut down your sugar to a mininimum. Maintain and active lifestyle. Carve out some time in your day to meditate and spend time with friends and family

For more nutritional way to take care of yourself:

 1)Lower dietary fat to between 10% to 20% of calories.

2)Eat more plant-based protein and eat less animal-based protein.

3)Drink 8 to 10 eight-ounce glasses of water a day.

4)Limit caffeine to no more than 1 to 2 cups a day.

5)Drink alcohol in moderation.

6)Limit nitrates and cured foods.

7)Decrease food additives.
